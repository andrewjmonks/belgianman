Arjuntina is a venue in verdant Stow, Massachusetts.

It’s run by Arjun Mathur.

Imma let you Finnish was a large concert held at Arjuntina on July 7, 2012. RTFM played an acoustic set, followed by Liz Wood, Scrub MC, DJ Kristöffer, Charlie Abbott, Christopher Knollmeyer, Kimbo Slice, and Luc Lacerte. Red Lips Big Hips closed the evening with a fantastic freestyle session, featuring Chris on the ones and the twos.
