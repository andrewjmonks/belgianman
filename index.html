Real avant-garde Kanye shit. Seriously. Dead-ass. follows four mashup albums, three live records, and a slew of performances. In it, Tokyo Megaplex combines original production and synthesis techniques with his extensive sampling experience. It's his first album of original music.

Tokyo Megaplex is Christopher Rutledge. He also releases music as DJ Kristöffer.

OM/NNI + //&~occur are two tracks by clown, exploring circular motion and pressure. They were recorded live using a custom-made wiimote-controlled sound generator.

clown is Andrew Monks, also a member of the Big Trucks, Belgian Man's original band.

DJ Wars is a project between Providence, RI DJs Remy Steele, of i am (noun), and Luc Lacerte. They're alternating back and forth creating heavy electro dubstep mixes, and these are the second four.

Belgian Man Records is a collective of artists based in lovely, historic Concord, MA.

We record music, film videos, make art, and play shows.
