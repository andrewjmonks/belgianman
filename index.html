So Sweet was directed by David Yanofsky for Kuh-Lida. The song features Faith Harding, who releases her own music on Stereocure as Novelty Daughter.

David is an artist based in Brooklyn, New York, and a founding member of Belgian Man Records. This is his fourth music video for Belgian Man, and his first for Kuh Lida

Bodily Banter, by EXIN.

Bodily Banter is the newest single by Boston-based EXIN Music, AKA David Dolan. It’s his first release on Belgian Man. He's quickly gaining traction in the Bean’s bass community.

△GILIT▽, by Fenn Macon.

△GILIT▽ is a six-track release by Worcester, MA based musician Fenn Macon. It’s his fifth Belgian Man release, following his February, 2012 album Cowards & Ghosts. The tracks on △GILIT▽ are based around samples from the recording sessions from Kittenmildew’s debut album, Liz Wood.
