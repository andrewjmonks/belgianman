Throbbin’ Da Banks is the new single by Owen McGarry, of i am (noun). It features a remix of i am (noun)’s unreleased track Then It Drops.

Owen is a musician, DJ, and industrial design student based out of Martha’s Vinyard, MA, and Providence, RI.

Money Hustard is the long-anticipated new album by Kuh-Lida. It’s the follow up to January’s Snacks.

Kuh-Lida is Myles Emmons, an electronic musician and beatmaker based out of Chicago, IL.

Released in July 2011, Fantastickal Worldz is the first EP by Countless Others.

Masters of melody, Charlie & Sam create beautiful pop music out of electronic soundscapes and Sam’s lyrics.

Fantastickal Worldz was just reviewed by Nick Mirin for Pop Veggies, you can find the review on their site.

Kuh-Lida is performing on Saturday at Art House Live, in West Los Angeles. The event is presented by Smoke Don't Smoke and also features Say Uhns, Lawrence Grey, Groundislava, and a SPECIAL SURPRISE GUEST.

You can find more details on the Facebook event.

A. Campbell Payne will be performing a piece titled Cloud Party as part of Figment Boston this Saturday on the Greenway.

It's a piece for solo performer and audience using the sky + shapes of clouds as a graphic score for performance. The shapes of the clouds and individual interpretations of how to sonify those shapes results in radically different performances depending on the weather, mood of the performers, geographical location, overall social vibe, etc.

You can find him by taking the T to South Station, then walk through the Greenway until you see Campbell. He'll be holding a sign, signing, and looking to the sky.
