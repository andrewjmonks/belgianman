Kuh-Lida is beatmaker Myles Emmons. He created Savage Casual during a summer residency with Stereocure.

Chris is a musician living in Lincoln, MA and Valencia, CA. His most recent project, Squares & Spheres, was released as two mini-albums: Squares, on Belgian Man, and Spheres, on Stereocure.

RYV is Charlie Abbott, also a member of the Big Trucks and formerly of Countless Others. REDYELLOWVIOLET is his first solo release. It features a remix of a track by Scrub MC.

Stereocure is a record label based in Los Angeles, California, and a partner of Belgian Man Records.

Kuh-Lida’s Savage Casual, and RYV’s REDYELLOWVIOLET were co-released by Stereocure and Belgian Man.
