The Big Trucks are Belgian Man’s original band.

Andrew Monks, David Yanofsky, & Charlie Abbott make up the Big Trucks.

The Big Trucks’ debut album, Big Trux Do Holiday $pirit Whil$t Gho$triding The Whip, was released on New Year’s Eve, 2010.

On New Year’s Eve 2011, the Big Trucks debuted a performance art piece, titled The Life, Death, Dreams, and Rebirth of Charlie Abbott. Musical backing for the piece was provided by Polar Bear (Owen McGarry & Aaron Gundy).
