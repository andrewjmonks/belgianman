Luc Lacerte

Luc Lacerte
DJ Wars
Mastering

Luc Lacerte is a musician, DJ, and mastering engineer from Providence, RI. He makes shit sound money.

Luc and fellow Providence, RI DJ Remy Steele make up dj wars. They’re alternating back and forth creating heavy electro dubstep mixes, and their first four have been released.

Luc mastered Liz Wood’s debut album, Liz Wood, Christopher Knollmeyer’s double-album Squares & Spheres, and RYV’s solo debut, REDYELLOWVIOLET.
