Fenn Macon is a guitarist, songwriter, and electronic musician based out of Worcester and historic North Andover, MA. He releases music under the name RTFM.

Fenn is currently working on an EP of remixes of the Liz Wood sessions. He’s also working on a new album of original music.

△GILIT▽ is a six-track release by Worcester, MA based musician Fenn Macon. It’s his fifth Belgian Man release, following his February, 2012 album Cowards & Ghosts. The tracks on △GILIT▽ are based around samples from the recording sessions from Kittenmildew’s debut album, Liz Wood.

Cowards & Ghosts is an EP inspired by Chris Onstad’s Achewood comics.

A quick mix, made half while learning Ableton and half using Logic.

Recorded over the course of 2011, Enemies, Closer! is RTFM’s debut album. It was inspired by hazy freshman dorms, motivation, and old flames.

inhalexile is RTFM’s debut EP, released in June 2011.

Words, by i am (noun) featured a remix by RTFM.
