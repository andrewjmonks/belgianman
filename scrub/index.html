Judd Mitchell AKA Scrub MC
Releases

Judd Mitchell is a rapper based in Burlington, VT and Belmont, MA.

Judd released his first album, the instrumental MOSH EP in December, 2010. He released An End To Every Sentence as Grey Agent in August, 2011. His first rap release was The Science Of Drifting Apart, in November, 2011.

Judd released the single Transmission in August, 2012, in anticipation of his December, 2012 debut mixtape, SCRVB

Judd raps as Blind Scientist and Scrub MC. He makes beats as Grey Agent and DJ Scrub.

SCRVB is the long-awaited debut mixtape by Scrub MC.
